# Picked Strategies

This document describes the four Morpho USDC vaults selected for the Tesseract demo vault. The selection focuses on conservative, diversified strategies managed by trusted vault operators.

## Strategy Selection Criteria

- **Conservative risk profile** - Established vaults with proven track records
- **Diversified exposure** - Different collateral types (BTC, ETH, altcoins, stablecoins)
- **High TVL** - Substantial total value locked indicating market trust
- **Trusted managers** - Reputable operators (Steakhouse, Gauntlet)
- **Yield optimization** - Balanced risk-return profiles

## Portfolio Rationale

These four vaults provide:

1. **Asset diversification** - BTC/ETH, altcoins, stablecoins, and multi-asset exposure
2. **Manager diversification** - Multiple trusted operators (Steakhouse, Gauntlet)
3. **Risk balance** - Mix of conservative blue-chip exposure and frontier opportunities
4. **Yield optimization** - Range from stable yields to higher-yielding strategies

The combination ensures that the Tesseract vault maintains a conservative overall profile while capturing diverse DeFi lending opportunities across the Morpho ecosystem.
